This book showcases uses altered photographs from Chile's Atacama Desert to illustrate Pablo Neruda's poem, "Ode to the Book". Neruda was elected senator for two provinces in the Atacama in 1945. The lunar landscapes of the desert highlight the mystical and imaginary nature of the poem.

This book was laser printed on brown paper.

Jungle is a text face designed for a fictional centennial edition of Upton Sinclair's The Jungle. The typeface is inspired by early 20th century ionic and clarendon types like Century and Ionic no. 5. It features dramatic ball terminals, slight wedge serifs, short decenders, and a very long em dash. The proportions were selected for optimum legibility.

This project began under the supervision of Cyrus Highsmith in his type design course and is a work in progress.

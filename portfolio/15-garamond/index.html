This hand-made accordion book illustrates the history and many cuts of the Garamond family of typefaces. The book takes pains to show the origins of eleven different cuts of Garamond, comparing the Claude Garamont specimen to the Jean Jannon punches.

The book is laser printed. It was created for a type course taught by Ben Shaykin.

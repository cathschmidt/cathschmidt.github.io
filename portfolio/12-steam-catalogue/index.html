The first catalogue of events, essays, and workshops by the RISD and Brown STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Math) student groups. The catalogue serves as both an archival document and a showcase for those interested in STEAM.

I'm proud to say that the group has continued the tradition. The latest catalogue is Catalogue Four.

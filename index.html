welcome to my page~

hi. my name is catherine leigh schmidt. i'm an artist, designer, and writer from the united states. my work focuses on economic media and post-capitalism. i'm based between new mexico and sachsen-anhalt, germany.

i help collaborators realize ultra high quality artefacts with a particular focus on typesetting.

Catherine Leigh Schmidt is a designer. She makes fonts, forms, code, and ideas. She got a BFA in Graphic Design from RISD in 2014. She was born in 1992.

Recent Projects

  1. Yatra, a Devanagari & Latin font based on Mumbai train signage. Started as Fulbright grant project. Released by Google.
  2. Bindery, a Jekyll template that outputs content into a book format. Hit ⌘P in a browser to make a book with page numbers & marginaelia. Fork & Go! With LTWP.

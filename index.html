welcome to my page~

hi. my name is catherine leigh schmidt. i'm an artist, designer, and writer from the united states. my work engages with theology, community building, and post-capitalist economic media. i'm based between london and sachsen-anhalt, germany.

as a designer i help collaborators realize light-weight projects with a particular focus on html, typesetting, and small print publications.
